Infrastructure

Education is the whole system of human training within the school, which molds and builds a man. To mold up the students, we have very good infrastructure facilities. We have a school library with rich collection of books, helping the students, to improve their general knowledge and language. To develop the skills in sports among the students, we do have a vast playground. We have fully equipped laboratories, each separate for High School, Higher Secondary School and Vocational Higher Secondary School.

High School and Higher Secondary Section have separate Physics lab, Chemistry lab, Computer lab and Biology lab. Vocational Higher Secondary School is equipped with the following four laboratories, rich with modern equipments:

  • Paramedical lab
  • Electronics lab
  • Computer lab
  • Type writing lab
