Located near Kammothu Bagavathy Temple, Panangad Vocational Higher Secondary School is the first English medium school to be established in Panangad, in 1919. It is a full-fledged Senior Secondary School (I-XII), along with Vocational Higher Secondary School, with over 2250 students. Co-curricular activities are given equal importance as curricular activities, in our Vidyalaya, thus helping the students to excel in all the required fields.
