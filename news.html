News and Events

We don’t need extra ordinary students, but we need ordinary students who do extra ordinary things. So, to develop such skills, we provide a very good opportunity for students. We the staffs, teachers along with our dear students, celebrate each and every special day, in our Vidyalaya in a grand way.

We don’t do different things, but we do things differently. Be it, the Environment day, or Water day, we celebrate each of them, together, thus giving the students an exposure to the society where they live in, and thus helping them love and respect their Mother Earth more.
